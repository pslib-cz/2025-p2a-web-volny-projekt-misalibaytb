BUILD STYLE

How I approach projects

I like projects with a clear backend shape, predictable data, and room to grow without making simple features painful. Static pages, focused APIs, and small interfaces keep the work practical and pleasant to use. I prefer building with privacy and control in mind, especially when projects collect or store user information. I consistently design projects that are scalable to accommodate increasing traffic.
